🌟 What is an Entrepreneur?

An entrepreneur is someone who:

  • 🌱 Sees a problem or opportunity
  • 💡 Comes up with a creative idea
  • 🛠️ Makes a plan to bring it to life
  • 🚀 Works hard and learns as they go
“You don’t have to be a grown-up to do great things.”

Starting your lemonade stand is more than selling drinks. You’re building:

  • ✅ Confidence
  • ✅ Creativity
  • ✅ Real-life skills like planning, budgeting, and leadership
