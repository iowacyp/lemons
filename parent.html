Dear Grown-Up

You play a key role in this adventure

Thanks for joining your child on this lemonade journey! This isn’t just about selling drinks—it’s about helping kids grow in confidence, creativity, and essential life skills.

  • 💛 Let them take the lead—even if it gets messy.
  • 🧠 Talk through what they learned after the big day.
  • 🎉 Celebrate their effort, not just their earnings.

Try practicing greetings, brainstorming flyer ideas together, and encouraging them to reflect afterward.

Mistakes are welcome—this is all part of the learning.

You’re not just supporting a lemonade stand. You’re raising a future leader.
